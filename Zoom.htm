General

Boost your Zoom efficiency with these essential shortcuts. From joining meetings to navigating the interface, these general shortcuts cover all the basics you need for a smoother Zoom experience.

Shortcuts Description
Ctrl + Shift + P Switch to Zoom Phone tab
Ctrl + Shift + M Minimize Zoom window
Ctrl + Shift + W Close Zoom window
Ctrl + Shift + T Toggle meeting controls
Ctrl + Shift + N New meeting
Ctrl + Shift + J Join meeting
Ctrl + Shift + V Start meeting with video
Ctrl + Shift + M Mute/unmute my microphone
Ctrl + Shift + U Show/hide participants panel
Ctrl + Shift + W Show/hide meeting window
Ctrl + Shift + K Copy meeting link
Ctrl + Shift + P Open Zoom Preferences/Settings

Meeting Control

Alt + I Invite
Ctrl + 2 Switch to Zoom Rooms minized
Alt + 1 Switch to Zoom Rooms enlarged
